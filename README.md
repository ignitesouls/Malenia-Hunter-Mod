# Malenia-Hunter-Mod

**Created by Shura & Psiphicode from Ignite**

Malenia is designed to hunt the player down and relentlessly pursue them across the Lands Between.

**Features**
- Malenia spawns directly on the player
- Malenia is unscaled
- If Malenia is killed, or if the player rests at a Site of Grace, a 45-second cooldown begins before she can spawn again
- Malenia instantly teleports to the player if they get too far away
- Malenia’s movement speed has been increased so she can better chase the player
- Most bosses have been modified so they neither deal damage to nor take damage from Malenia
- Currently, only Phase 1 Malenia is used for the chase

**Install & Play**
- Download and Unzip `Malenia.Hunter.Full.Mod.zip`
- Open `launchmod_eldenring.bat`

**Modding Details**
The DLL handles spawning Malenia, managing the cooldown, and warping her to the player’s position.

The movement speed change was made with DS Animation Studio.

All other changes were made through event scripts and Smithbox.

- DS Animation Studio: https://github.com/Meowmaritus/DSAnimStudio
- DarkScript3: https://github.com/AinTunez/DarkScript3
- Smithbox: https://github.com/vawser/Smithbox
